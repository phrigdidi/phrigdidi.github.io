Gluten-Free Vegan Macaroni Casserole

Recipe adapted from Loving It Vegan. This baked macaroni casserole uses coconut milk, nutritional yeast, and clever seasonings to create a "cheese" sauce that fools most folks! With Winco Chickpea Elbow Pasta and a gluten-free, nut-free roux (using corn starch) and a crunchy topping made from crushed corn flakes, this recipe is the ultimate people-pleaser!

Ingredients

  • Cheese Sauce:
    • 2 cans coconut milk (have a 3rd on hand if needed)
    • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
    • 1 tbsp corn starch (for roux)
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1 tsp onion powder
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric (for color)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pasta:
    • 1 box Winco Chickpea Elbow Pasta (or other gluten-free pasta)
  • Topping:
    • 1 cup crushed corn flakes
    • 1 tbsp olive oil or melted vegan butter

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the pasta according to package instructions, but remove it when it's slightly chewy (gluten-free noodles overcook easily!). Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the cheese sauce:
    1. In a saucepan, whisk coconut milk and corn starch over medium heat until thickened.
    2. Add nutritional yeast, garlic powder, onion powder, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Stir until smooth and creamy.
  4. Combine the cooked pasta and cheese sauce in a large bowl, mixing well.
  5. Prepare the topping: Toss crushed corn flakes with olive oil or melted vegan butter.
  6. Transfer the pasta mixture to a greased baking dish. Sprinkle the corn flake topping evenly over the top.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the topping is golden and the casserole is bubbly.
  8. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
